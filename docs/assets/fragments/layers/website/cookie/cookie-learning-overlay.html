What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that help a website remember settings, maintain security, and work correctly.

Essential cookies protect security

Security & abuse prevention, session continuity, and interface integrity cookies are always required to keep Neuroartan safe and functional.

Optional cookies improve experience

Analytics cookies measure performance and usage insights. Experience cookies remember preferences and support smoother return visits.

Your choice remains clear

Neuroartan separates required cookies from optional ones so you can make informed decisions about which cookies to allow.

Why we use them

We use cookies to keep Neuroartan secure, remember consent choices, and improve the website when allowed.

Security & abuse prevention

Required cookies help protect Neuroartan from misuse and harmful activity, keeping the website secure for all visitors.

Session continuity & interface integrity

Required cookies keep essential sessions working and maintain stable language, consent, and basic interface settings.

Optional improvement insights

Analytics cookies measure performance and usage insights. Experience cookies remember preferences and support smoother return visits. These are optional and only used with your consent.
