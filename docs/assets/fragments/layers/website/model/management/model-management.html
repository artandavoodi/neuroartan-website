Training protocol

Training is staged through source authorization, acquisition, normalization, retrieval preparation, readiness assessment, and runtime activation.

  1. Authorize owner-approved sources.
  2. Ingest and preserve raw source provenance.
  3. Normalize, segment, embed, and assess readiness.
