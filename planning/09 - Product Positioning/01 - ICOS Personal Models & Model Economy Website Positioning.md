# ICOS Personal Models & Model Economy Website Positioning

---

## I. Purpose

This document defines safe website language for FSC-T-0007.

---

## II. Approved Doctrine

> AI absorbs operational labor while the human retains sovereign authorship, ownership, and direction.

---

## III. Approved Website Language

- personal model;
- trainable digital model;
- continuity profile;
- verified model identity;
- model birth identity;
- user-directed cognitive asset;
- future monetization potential;
- governed model economy;
- AI that works under your direction.

---

## IV. Prohibited Website Language

- guaranteed income;
- passive income machine;
- conscious AI;
- digital personhood;
- model rights;
- AI doctor;
- AI lawyer;
- autonomous worker without user control;
- posthumous model that earns forever.

## V. Homepage-Safe Copy

> Create a personal model. Train it with your knowledge. Direct its work.

Expanded:

> ICOS lets each person create a personal model that learns from their voice, memory, sources, and direction — a model that can support continuity, work, and future governed economic participation.

## VI. Publication Gate

No FSC-T-0007 website copy may be published before Founder, Governance, Legal, Product, WSDA, and final copy review.
